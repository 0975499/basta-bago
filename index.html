My Hobbies.

Playing Piano

Way back when I was a kid. My dad used to play piano. I watch him play while he is talking about basic notes so that I can remember and try them on my own. He used to tell me that learning piano and having a mentor is quite expensive, but the mentor in their province teaches my dad for free. He is lucky I would say, and I see in my dad the eagerness to learn that time, while he is telling a story. I used to have a hard time understanding the notes, but my dad was always by my side while I played the piano, it became our bonding time. My favorite piece that my dad used plays is the river flow in you by Yiruma. The piano served as a symbol of one's social status.
